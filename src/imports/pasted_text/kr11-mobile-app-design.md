Final Quantity and Player Count Rule:
This is very important.

Quantity directly affects player count.

1 quantity = 1 player count.
A complete team requires 11 total players.
Team count is calculated from the total quantity selected across all companies.

Examples:
- RELIANCE with quantity 5 = 5 players
- ZOMATO with quantity 2 = 2 players
- TATA MOTORS with quantity 1 = 1 player

So:
RELIANCE Qty 5 + ZOMATO Qty 2 + TATA MOTORS Qty 1 = Team 8 / 11

Important:
Quantity affects both:
1. Player count
2. Coins required

Coins Required = Company Value × Quantity
